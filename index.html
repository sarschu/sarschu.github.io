Who I Am

I've journeyed through the digital world with a PhD in Computational Linguistics in one hand and a programmer's toolkit in the other. From crunching code as a Machine Learning Engineer to orchestrating projects as a Program Manager and steering tech teams as an Engineering Manager, I've surfed the tech startup wave with style.

However, it's not just algorithms and data that fuel my passion. My true calling lies in connecting with individuals like you. I understand the journey you're on because I've navigated my own, and I'm here to guide you with both professionalism and a genuine understanding of the human experience.

Right now, I am training to become a certified ACC-level coach with the Internatioal Coaching Federation.

My love for nature brought me from Germany to Ireland - a country wild, rough and widely untouched. Even though I love to connect virtually and believe in a world that is digitally connected, I love to use nature as a facilitator in face-to-face sessions.

What I Do

I'm here to help you understand your own potential and rewrite your story in lines of success, happiness, and personal fulfillment. So, let's embark on a journey together – one where we blend the art of communication with the science of achievement.

Think of me as your guide through the intricacies of life's code – whether it's in tech or the vast universe of personal development. Let's break the algorithms that hold you back, optimize your life for peak performance, and debug any obstacles that come your way.

I will help you set your goals, help you understand how to reach them and support you in making sure that you know when you achieved them.
